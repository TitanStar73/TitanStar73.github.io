Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 26 November, 2024 Introduction Ion Toad (and all its apps) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data. Data Collection and Usage Ion Toad does not collect, store, or process any personal or user data. Ion Toad performs the following operations: Pinging a website to fetch publicly available data on our github repo. Displaying this data using the local time on your device. The local time is used only for display purposes and is not shared with any other service or entity. Third-Party Plugins Ion Toad uses Apache Cordova and its plugins to enable its features. While these plugins provide essential functionality for Ion Toad, they do not collect or transmit any personal data unless explicitly stated otherwise in their respective documentation. No Sharing of Information Since the App does not collect any user data, no information is shared with third parties. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here, and the effective date will be updated accordingly. Thank you for your trust in Ion Toad.
